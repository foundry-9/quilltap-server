# Left Sidebar

> **[Open this page in Quilltap](/)**

The left sidebar is your main navigation hub for accessing all parts of Quilltap. It is always visible on the left side of the screen in a compact, icon-only format that gives you quick access to everything without taking up space from your content area.

## Sidebar Sections

### Projects

At the top of the sidebar, you'll find your **Projects** section. This shows:

- **Your active projects** --- Listed in order of recent activity
- **Project icons** --- Click any project icon to view its details
- **View All link** --- See a complete list of all your projects

Projects help you organize related characters and conversations into coherent workflows.

### Files

Below Projects, the **Files** section displays:

- **Recently accessed files** --- Your most recently used files appear here
- **File icons** --- Click to open or preview a file
- **View All link** --- Browse your complete file library

This is handy for quickly accessing resources you're actively working with.

### Characters

The **Characters** section shows:

- **Your favorite characters** --- The characters you use most frequently
- **Character avatars** --- Visual identification for each character
- **View All link** --- See your complete character roster

Characters are the heart of Quilltap --- these are the personalities you create and interact with. Click any character avatar to view their profile or start chatting with them.

### Chats

The **Chats** section displays:

- **Recent conversations** --- Your most active chats appear at the top
- **Chat icons** --- Click to open a conversation
- **Unread indicators** --- See which chats have new messages
- **View All link** --- Access your complete chat history

This is your quick access to ongoing conversations and past interactions.

## Using the Sidebar

### Icon-Only Navigation

The sidebar displays icons for each section and item. Hover over any icon to see a tooltip with the item's name. Click any icon to navigate directly to that item.

### Search and Quick Actions

- Use the **search bar** at the top of the page to quickly find items across all sections
- Use **Quick Actions** on the homepage to create new projects, characters, or chats
- Click section titles to jump to "View All" for a complete list

## Sidebar Footer

At the bottom of the sidebar, you'll find navigation links:

- **Foundry** --- Open The Foundry hub to manage settings, tools, and system configuration
- **Profile** --- Access your account settings
- **Help** --- Access documentation and guides

The **Foundry** link is your single entry point for all settings and system management. See [The Foundry](settings.md) for details on what you'll find there.

## Tips for Efficient Navigation

1. **Use tooltips** --- Hover over sidebar icons to see what they represent
2. **Check for unread items** --- Visual indicators show you where there's activity
3. **Use search for speed** --- The search bar at the top of the page is the fastest way to find any item
4. **Access Foundry from the footer** --- All settings and tools are one click away through the Foundry link

The sidebar keeps navigation compact and accessible so you can stay focused on your writing and conversations.
