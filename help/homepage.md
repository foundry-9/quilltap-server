# Homepage

> **[Open this page in Quilltap](/)**

Welcome to your Quilltap dashboard! The homepage is your central hub for quick access to your creative work and getting started with new projects.

## What You'll See

### Welcome Greeting

At the top of the page, you'll see a personalized welcome message with your name. This gives you a quick reminder of what you can do next.

### Quick Actions

Below the welcome section, you'll find quick action buttons to help you get started:

- **New Chat** — Start a conversation with one of your characters
- **New Project** — Create a new creative project
- **New Character** — Add a new character to your roster
- **New File** — Upload or create a new file for your projects

### Recent Chats

This section displays your most recently active conversations, showing:

- **Chat title** — The name of your chat
- **Participants** — Character avatars showing who's involved
- **Last updated** — When the chat was last active
- **Message count** — How many messages are in the conversation

You can click any recent chat to jump back into the conversation, or hover over a chat to hide it if you want to keep your list cleaner.

### Projects

Your creative projects are listed here with:

- **Project name** — The title of your project
- **Member count** — How many characters are part of this project
- **Activity indicator** — Shows recently active projects at the top

Click on a project to explore its details and manage its characters.

### Favorite Characters

This section shows your favorite characters at a glance:

- **Character avatar** — Their profile image
- **Character name** — Displayed below the avatar
- **Quick access** — Click to view their profile or start a new chat with them

## Navigation Tips

- Use the **sidebar** on the left to navigate between different sections of Quilltap
- Use the **search bar** at the top to quickly find chats, characters, or other content
- Use the **width toggle** button to adjust how much space the main content area takes up
- Your recent chats section shows your most active conversations, making it easy to pick up where you left off

## Getting Started

1. **Create your first character** using the Quick Actions or the sidebar
2. **Start a chat** with your character to begin creating stories
3. **Organize your work** into projects to keep related content together
4. **Use the search bar** to quickly find anything across your workspace

Need help with a specific feature? Check the help resources for the sidebar, search, or other components.
