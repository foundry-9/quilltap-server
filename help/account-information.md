# Account Information

> **[Open this page in Quilltap](/profile)**

Your Account Information section displays system details about your Quilltap account. This information is read-only and helps you understand your account status.

## What's Displayed Here?

The Account Information section shows important system-generated details about your account. Each field is explained below.

### User ID

Your unique account identifier in Quilltap's system.

**What it is:**

- A unique identifier generated when your account was created
- Used internally by Quilltap to track your account
- Remains the same for the entire lifetime of your account
- Formatted as a long alphanumeric string

**Why it matters:**

- Useful for technical support when reporting issues
- Identifies your account in system logs
- Required if Quilltap support needs to help with account-specific issues

**Copy to Clipboard:**

- Click the copy icon next to your User ID to copy it to your clipboard
- A checkmark appears briefly confirming the copy was successful
- Paste it when contacting support or for record-keeping

### Username

Your login username for Quilltap.

**What it is:**

- The name you use to log in to your account
- Set when your account is created
- Different from your Display Name (which you can customize)

**Why it matters:**

- Your unique identifier for authentication
- Used in URL slugs and system references
- Remains constant even if you change your Display Name

**Note:** Usernames typically cannot be changed after account creation.

### Account Created

The date and time when your Quilltap account was first created.

**Format:**

- Displayed in your local date/time format
- Shows both the date (Month Day, Year) and time (HH:MM in 12 or 24-hour format)
- Timezone is based on your browser/device settings

**What it tells you:**

- How long you've been using Quilltap
- Useful reference point if you need to track your account history
- Appears in support records if you contact help

### Last Updated

The date and time when your profile was most recently modified.

**Format:**

- Displayed in your local date/time format
- Shows both the date and time
- Automatically updated whenever you change profile information

**What changes this:**

- Editing your Display Name
- Changing your Email Address
- Updating your Profile Avatar
- Any other profile changes

**What it tells you:**

- When you last made profile changes
- Whether your profile information is current
- Can help you track your account activity

## Understanding Your Account Details

**This information is read-only** - You cannot edit any of these fields directly. They're system-generated information maintained by Quilltap.

**To modify your account:**

- **Display Name & Email:** Edit in the Profile Settings section above
- **Avatar:** Change in the Profile Settings section above
- **Username & User ID:** These cannot be changed after account creation
- **Account Created & Last Updated:** These dates update automatically

## Using This Information

**For Personal Records:**

- Note your User ID for your records
- Check the "Account Created" date to remember when you joined
- The "Last Updated" timestamp helps you verify when you last changed your profile

**For Support:**

- Provide your User ID when contacting Quilltap support
- Reference these dates when describing when changes were made
- Use this information to verify you're looking at your current account

**Privacy Considerations:**

- Your User ID is system information
- Your Username and account dates are part of your account profile
- All this information is private to your account

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I change my Username?**
No, your username is set when your account is created and cannot be changed. If you need to change how you appear to others, update your Display Name in the Profile Settings section instead.

**Why does my User ID look so long?**
User IDs in Quilltap are unique identifiers that ensure no two accounts have the same ID. This format is secure and reliable.

**What if the "Last Updated" date is old?**
This just means you haven't made any changes to your profile settings recently. It's not a problem - you can update your profile anytime.

**Can I change when my account was created?**
No, the "Account Created" date is automatically set when your account is first created and cannot be modified. It's a historical record of when you joined Quilltap.

**Is my User ID secret?**
Your User ID is not sensitive information like a password. It's typically safe to share with support, but you don't need to provide it to other users unless they specifically ask for it.
