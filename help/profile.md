# Your Profile

> **[Open this page in Quilltap](/profile)**

Your profile page is your personal account hub where you can manage your account information and customize how you appear throughout Quilltap.

## What's on Your Profile?

Your profile page contains three main sections:

### Profile Settings (Editable)

The top section allows you to customize your personal information:

- **Display Name** - The name shown throughout the application when you interact with content
- **Email Address** - Your account email, which may be used for account recovery
- **Profile Avatar** - A visual representation of you displayed in the app interface

### Account Information (Read-Only)

The middle section shows system information about your account:

- **User ID** - Your unique account identifier (with copy-to-clipboard button)
- **Username** - Your login username
- **Account Created** - The date and time your account was created
- **Last Updated** - The date and time your profile was last modified

### Data Directory

The bottom section shows where Quilltap stores your data:

- **Location** - The full path to your data directory (with copy-to-clipboard button)
- **Configuration** - Shows whether the path is from an environment variable or platform default
- **Platform** - Your operating system (macOS, Windows, Linux, or Docker)
- **Open in File Browser** - Button to open the directory in your system's file browser (not available in Docker environments)

## Accessing Your Profile

To access your profile page, look for the profile menu in the top navigation or footer of the app. Your profile page can typically be found at `/profile` within the app.

## Why Update Your Profile?

**Display Name** - Keeping this updated helps you maintain consistency throughout Quilltap. Your name appears when you create characters, upload files, and participate in the system.

**Email Address** - An up-to-date email ensures you can recover your account if needed and receive important account notifications.

**Avatar Image** - A profile picture personalizes your account and makes your identity instantly recognizable in the application.
