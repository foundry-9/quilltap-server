# Choosing a Profile Avatar

> **[Open this page in Quilltap](/profile)**

Your profile avatar is a visual representation of you in Quilltap. Here's how to select and manage it.

## What's a Profile Avatar?

A profile avatar is an image displayed next to your name throughout the application. It helps personalize your account and makes you instantly recognizable in the app interface.

Your avatar typically appears:

- On your Profile page
- In user menus in the navigation
- In any interface showing account information
- Next to your name when you create or interact with content

## Avatar Requirements

To use an image as your profile avatar:

- **Source:** The image must be from your Quilltap image library
- **Format:** Supported image formats (JPEG, PNG, etc.) that are already in your library
- **Size:** Images are automatically displayed at avatar size (96x96 pixels on the profile page)

**If you don't have images yet:**
If you don't see any images in the avatar selector, you'll need to upload some first:

1. Go to the Files section in Quilltap
2. Upload images you want to use
3. Return to your Profile Settings to select one as your avatar

## How to Select an Avatar

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Go to your Profile page** - Access your profile from the app's user menu or by navigating to `/profile`

2. **Look for the avatar section** - In the Profile Settings section, you'll see a large circular image (or a user icon placeholder if no avatar is set)

3. **Click the Change button** - You can either:
   - Click the blue **Change avatar** button text below the avatar
   - Click the blue pencil/edit icon button in the bottom-right corner of the avatar circle

4. **Browse available images** - A modal window opens showing images from your library
   - Scroll through available images
   - Click on the image you want to use

5. **Image is applied** - Your new avatar is immediately set and the modal closes automatically

6. **See your avatar everywhere** - Your new profile picture appears throughout the app within seconds

## Changing Your Avatar

**To change to a different image:**

- Simply follow the steps above again
- Select a new image from the modal
- Your avatar updates immediately

**To remove your avatar:**

- Select "no avatar" or clear the selection in the avatar picker
- This returns your profile to showing the default user icon placeholder

## Avatar Tips

**Size and Display:**

- Avatars are displayed as circles in most places
- Images are automatically cropped to fit the circular frame
- Square or circular images work best
- Very wide or very tall images may be partially hidden

**Privacy:**

- Your avatar is visible to anyone viewing your profile
- Choose images you're comfortable sharing
- You can change your avatar anytime

**Consistency:**

- Keep the same avatar to maintain visual recognition
- Changing your avatar frequently might confuse other users
- You might want to use a consistent profile picture across apps

## Troubleshooting

**I don't see any images in the avatar selector**

- This means you haven't uploaded any images to your library yet
- Go to your Files section and upload some images first
- Return to your Profile Settings and try again

**My avatar isn't showing up**

- Try refreshing the page (Cmd+R on Mac, Ctrl+R on Windows/Linux)
- The avatar may take a moment to propagate throughout the app
- Clear your browser cache if the issue persists

**I want to use an image that's not in the list**

- The avatar selector only shows images you've uploaded to your library
- Upload the new image in your Files section
- Return to your Profile Settings and it should appear

**My avatar looks cropped or stretched**

- Avatars are displayed as circles and may crop square images
- Consider uploading a properly square image for best results
- Try a different image if the current one doesn't display well

**The avatar picker modal won't open**

- Make sure you have at least one image in your library
- Try refreshing the page
- Check that you have permission to upload and select images
