# Width Toggle Button

> **[Open this page in Quilltap](/)**

The width toggle button allows you to switch between two different content width layouts, giving you control over how much screen space your main content uses.

## What the Button Does

Located in the **top right** of the page next to the search bar, this button toggles between:

- **Narrow layout** — Content is constrained to 800 pixels wide, keeping text and elements centered
- **Wide layout** — Content expands to fill your entire screen width (100%) for maximum workspace

## When to Use Each Layout

### Use Narrow Layout When

- **Writing long text** — Narrow columns are easier to read and write in
- **Reading content** — A narrower text width reduces eye strain during long reading sessions
- **Working on detailed editing** — The constrained width can help you focus on one thing
- **Using a large monitor** — More space on the sides can feel overwhelming
- **You prefer traditional book-like reading** — Similar to a book's page width

### Use Wide Layout When

- **Viewing lists or tables** — See more items at once without scrolling
- **Working with multiple columns** — Spreadsheet-like content benefits from the extra width
- **Reviewing project overviews** — See more of your dashboard at a glance
- **You have limited vertical space** — Using horizontal space prevents excessive scrolling
- **Working on a small screen** — Maximize every pixel of your display

## Finding the Button

The width toggle button is located:

- **Position** — Top right corner of the page
- **Icon style** — Shows arrows (expand/compress)
- **Visibility** — Only appears on screens 1000px or wider where the toggle actually makes a difference

On very small screens (under 1000px), the button may be hidden since the layout automatically adapts to smaller viewports.

## Toggle Icon Meanings

- **Outward-pointing arrows** — You're currently in narrow mode; click to expand to wide layout
- **Inward-pointing arrows** — You're currently in wide mode; click to compress to narrow layout

The icon changes to show you which direction you'll go when you click.

## Your Preference is Saved

- Quilltap **remembers your choice** — The last layout you selected is used when you return
- **Per session or per device** — Your preference is saved in your browser
- **Switch anytime** — You can toggle between layouts as many times as you want without restrictions

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Try both layouts** — Experiment with each to see which feels better for your workflow
2. **Switch for different tasks** — Use narrow for writing, wide for reviewing
3. **Combine with sidebar resizing** — You can also resize the left sidebar to customize your layout further
4. **Test on your screen size** — What works best depends on your monitor size and resolution

The width toggle gives you flexibility to shape Quilltap's interface to match your working style!
