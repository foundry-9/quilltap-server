# Left Sidebar

> **[Open this page in Quilltap](/)**

The left sidebar is your main navigation hub for accessing all parts of Quilltap. It's always available on the left side of the screen and can be expanded or collapsed to fit your workflow.

## Sidebar Sections

### Projects

At the top of the sidebar, you'll find your **Projects** section. This shows:

- **Your active projects** — Listed in order of recent activity
- **Project names** — Click any project to view its details
- **View All link** — See a complete list of all your projects

Projects help you organize related characters and conversations into coherent workflows.

### Files

Below Projects, the **Files** section displays:

- **Recently accessed files** — Your most recently used files appear here
- **File names** — Click to open or preview a file
- **View All link** — Browse your complete file library

This is handy for quickly accessing resources you're actively working with.

### Characters

The **Characters** section shows:

- **Your favorite characters** — The characters you use most frequently
- **Character avatars** — Visual identification for each character
- **Character names** — Click to view their profile or start chatting with them
- **View All link** — See your complete character roster

Characters are the heart of Quilltap—these are the personalities you create and interact with.

### Chats

The **Chats** section displays:

- **Recent conversations** — Your most active chats appear at the top
- **Chat titles** — Click to open a conversation
- **Unread indicators** — See which chats have new messages
- **View All link** — Access your complete chat history

This is your quick access to ongoing conversations and past interactions.

## Using the Sidebar

### Expand or Collapse

- Click the **collapse button** (hamburger menu) at the top of the sidebar to toggle between expanded and collapsed views
- In **collapsed mode**, you'll see icon-only navigation for faster switching
- In **expanded mode**, you'll see full names and more details

### Resize the Sidebar

- Position your cursor at the right edge of the sidebar until it changes to a resize cursor
- Drag left or right to adjust the sidebar width to your preference
- Your preferred width is saved automatically

### Search and Quick Actions

- Use the **search bar** at the top of the page to quickly find items across all sections
- Use **Quick Actions** on the homepage to create new projects, characters, or chats
- Click section titles to jump to "View All" for a complete list

## Profile Menu

At the bottom of the sidebar, you'll find your **Profile Menu**:

- **Your profile** — Click to access your account settings
- **Settings** — Configure Quilltap to match your preferences
- **Help** — Access documentation and guides
- **Sign out** — Log out of Quilltap

## Tips for Efficient Navigation

1. **Keep frequently used items visible** — Resize the sidebar to show the items you access most
2. **Use collapsed mode on smaller screens** — Save space for your content on mobile devices
3. **Collapse when you need more space** — Switch to icon-only mode when you're focused on writing or chatting
4. **Check the sidebar for unread items** — Visual indicators show you where there's activity

The sidebar adapts to your workflow—experiment with different widths and collapse states to find what works best for you!
