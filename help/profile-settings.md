# Profile Settings

> **[Open this page in Quilltap](/profile)**

The Profile Settings section lets you customize your personal information and choose how you appear in Quilltap.

## What You Can Edit

### Display Name

Your display name is the name shown throughout Quilltap when you interact with content, create characters, or use features in the application.

**How to edit your Display Name:**

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Look for the **Display Name** field in the Profile Settings section
3. Click the text field and enter your preferred name (up to 100 characters)
4. The name is displayed beneath your profile avatar on the profile page
5. Click **Save Changes** to apply your update

**Tips for Display Names:**

- You can use any name you prefer - it doesn't have to match your real name
- Special characters, spaces, and emojis are supported
- The name is optional - if you leave it blank, your username will be used instead
- Changes appear throughout the app after you save

### Email Address

Your email address can be used for account recovery and may be used for important account notifications.

**How to edit your Email:**

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Find the **Email Address** field in the Profile Settings section
3. Click the text field and enter your email address
4. The email field shows the current email (or "No email set" if none is configured)
5. Click **Save Changes** to apply your update

**Tips for Email:**

- Email must be in a valid format (e.g., <user@example.com>)
- Changing your email doesn't log you out
- Consider using a reliable email address you have access to
- An email helps with account recovery if you forget your password

### Profile Avatar

Your profile avatar is the image displayed next to your name throughout the application, helping others recognize you at a glance.

**How to change your Avatar:**

1. Go to your Profile page
2. In the Profile Settings section, look for the large circular avatar image (or user icon placeholder)
3. There are two ways to change it:
   - **Option A:** Click the **Change avatar** button below the avatar display
   - **Option B:** Click the blue pencil/edit icon in the corner of the avatar circle
4. A modal window opens showing available avatar images
5. Browse and select the image you want to use as your profile avatar
6. The selected image immediately becomes your new avatar
7. The modal closes automatically and your avatar updates throughout the app

**Avatar Tips:**

- Avatars are images from your image library in Quilltap
- Only images you've uploaded or have access to can be used as avatars
- Your avatar appears in:
  - Your profile page
  - User menus in the app interface
  - Any other places showing account information
- You can change your avatar as often as you'd like
- Removing an avatar goes back to the default user icon placeholder

## Saving Your Changes

When you've made any changes to your Display Name or Email Address:

1. The **Save Changes** button appears in the Profile Settings section
2. The button is only active when you have unsaved changes
3. Click **Save Changes** to apply all your updates at once
4. You'll see a success message confirming your profile was updated
5. If there's an error (like an invalid email), you'll see an error message

**What happens when saving:**

- Your changes are sent to the server
- A confirmation appears: "Profile updated successfully"
- All changes take effect immediately throughout the app
- Your profile is now updated with the new information

## Editing Tips

**Making Multiple Changes:**

- You can edit your Display Name and Email at the same time
- Make all your changes, then click **Save Changes** once to save them all together

**Going Back:**

- If you make changes but want to discard them, just reload the page
- Your changes won't be saved until you click the **Save Changes** button

**Troubleshooting:**

- **Email validation error:** Make sure your email is in the correct format (<user@example.com>)
- **Save button is disabled:** This means you haven't made any changes to the fields
- **Update failed:** Check your internet connection and try again
- **Avatar won't change:** Make sure you have images in your library; if not, upload some first
